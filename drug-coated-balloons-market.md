<p><p>Drug-Coated Balloons (DCBs) are specialized medical devices used primarily in the treatment of cardiovascular diseases. The targeted delivery of drugs directly to the site of treatment enhances efficacy while minimizing systemic exposure.</p><p>The Drug-Coated Balloons Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.1% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing prevalence of coronary artery disease and peripheral artery disease worldwide.

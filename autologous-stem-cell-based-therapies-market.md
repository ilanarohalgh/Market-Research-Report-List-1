<p><p>The autologous stem cell-based therapies market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Embryonic Stem Cells** are derived from early-stage embryos and possess the ability to differentiate into any cell type, making them highly versatile. </p><p>2. **Resident Cardiac Stem Cells** are naturally occurring stem cells within the heart that aid in repair and regeneration, especially after injury. </p><p>3. **Umbilical Cord Blood Stem Cells** are collected from umbilical cords post-birth, rich in hematopoietic stem cells, useful for treating various blood disorders. Each type offers unique therapeutic potential in regenerative medicine.</p></p>

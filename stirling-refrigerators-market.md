<p><p>Stirling refrigerators are categorized into two main market types: integral and split type. Integral type systems feature the compressor and cold-producing components in a single unit, making them compact and suitable for smaller applications. Conversely, split type systems separate these components, allowing for more flexibility in installation and larger cooling capacities. Both types are efficient and provide reliable cooling, catering to diverse needs in industries such as medical, aerospace, and industrial cooling processes.</p></p>

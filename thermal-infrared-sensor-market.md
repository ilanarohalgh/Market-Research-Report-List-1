<p><strong>The Thermal Infrared Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photon Detection</li><li>Thermal Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal infrared sensor market is primarily divided into two main types: photon detection and thermal detection. Photon detection sensors, also known as photonic sensors, operate by detecting individual photons to capture thermal radiation across various wavelengths, providing high sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, thermal detection sensors measure changes in temperature by monitoring the emitted infrared radiation from objects, making them suitable for applications like temperature measurement and surveillance. Both types play crucial roles in diverse sectors, including defense, automotive, and industrial applications.</p></p>

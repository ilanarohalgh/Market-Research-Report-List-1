<p><p>The Heat Control Windshield Market encompasses two primary types: Heated Wire Windshields and Heated Coated Windshields. Heated Wire Windshields utilize embedded wires to generate warmth, effectively preventing ice and fog buildup. In contrast, Heated Coated Windshields feature a transparent conductive coating that evenly distributes heat across the surface, offering improved visibility and faster defogging. Both technologies enhance safety and comfort in adverse weather conditions, catering to the automotive industry's growing demand for improved visibility and enhanced driving experience.</p></p>

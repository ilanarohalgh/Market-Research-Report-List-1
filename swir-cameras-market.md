<p><p>The SWIR cameras market is divided into two main types: SWIR Area Cameras and SWIR Linear Cameras. SWIR Area Cameras capture two-dimensional images, making them suitable for various applications such as industrial monitoring, surveillance, and spectroscopy. In contrast, SWIR Linear Cameras are designed for line-scan imaging, capturing one-dimensional data over a larger area, which is ideal for applications like conveyor belt inspections and material sorting. Both types utilize short-wave infrared technology to provide enhanced imaging capabilities in challenging conditions.</p></p>

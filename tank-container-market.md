<p><p>The tank container market includes various specialized types designed for specific cargo needs. Reefer tank containers are temperature-controlled for perishable goods, while heated tank containers maintain high temperatures for substances like wax and oils. Super-insulated tank containers minimize heat transfer for cryogenic liquids. Swap body tank containers combine the features of tank and dry freight containers for versatile transport. Lastly, common tank containers are standard vessels for transporting liquids and bulk materials across industries, ensuring safety and compliance.</p></p>

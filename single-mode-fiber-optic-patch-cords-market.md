<p><p>Single-mode fiber optic patch cords are essential for high-speed, long-distance data transmission. The market is segmented into several types: FC patch cords, known for their screw-on connection and low-loss, are ideal for high-power applications; SC patch cords feature a push-pull design, making them easy to connect and disconnect; ST patch cords utilize a bayonet-style connector, suitable for legacy systems; and LC patch cords, with a small form factor, are preferred in high-density environments. Each type caters to specific connectivity needs in various applications.</p></p>

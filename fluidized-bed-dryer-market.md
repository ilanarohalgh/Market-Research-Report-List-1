<p><p>The fluidized bed dryer market comprises several types, including vibrated fluid beds, which enhance particle movement for uniform drying; fluid-bed granulation, combining drying and granulation processes; spouted bed dryers that utilize a strong upward airflow to maintain particles in a nearly suspended state; mechanically agitated fluid-bed dryers that improve mixing; centrifugal fluid-bed dryers using centrifugal force for enhanced material handling; and fluidized spray dryers that atomize liquid feeds for efficient drying. Each type is tailored for specific applications in industries like pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals.</p></p>

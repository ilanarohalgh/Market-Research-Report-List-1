<p><p>The thermofusible film market encompasses various materials tailored for diverse applications. TPU, or thermoplastic polyurethanes, offers flexibility and durability, making it ideal for medical and automotive uses. EVA, or ethylene vinyl acetate, is known for its transparency and elasticity, commonly used in packaging. Polyamide (PA) provides excellent mechanical properties and thermal resistance, suitable for industrial applications. PES, or polyethersulfone, offers high-temperature stability, while polyolefin (PO) films provide cost-effectiveness and moisture resistance, widely used in various industries.</p></p>

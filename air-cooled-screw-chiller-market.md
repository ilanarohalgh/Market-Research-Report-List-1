<p><p>The air-cooled screw chiller market is segmented into two main types based on adjustment mechanisms: electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. Electrodeless adjustment allows for smooth modulation of the chiller's capacity in response to varying cooling demands, enhancing efficiency and energy savings. In contrast, sectional adjustment features discrete steps of operation, providing simpler control for specific applications. Both types cater to different operational needs, with electrodeless adjustment favoring precise control and sectional adjustment offering straightforward manageability in cooling processes.</p></p>

Coordinated Multi-Point (CoMP) technology improves signal coverage and data rates by synchronizing multiple transmission points. Relay technology extends coverage and capacity by using intermediate nodes to connect users to the main network. Carrier aggregation technology combines multiple frequency bands to increase bandwidth and throughput. Other markets may include features like advanced MIMO, load balancing, and heterogeneous networks, all aimed at optimizing mobile communication efficiency.</p></p>

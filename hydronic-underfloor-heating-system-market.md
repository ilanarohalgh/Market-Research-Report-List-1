<p><p>The hydronic underfloor heating system market comprises two main types: dry water floor heating and wet water floor heating. Dry water floor heating utilizes a series of electric heating cables placed beneath the flooring, offering easier installation but often higher operational costs. In contrast, wet water floor heating circulates heated water through pipes embedded in the floor, providing efficient warmth and lower energy costs. Both systems enhance comfort and energy efficiency in residential and commercial spaces but differ in installation and maintenance requirements.</p></p>

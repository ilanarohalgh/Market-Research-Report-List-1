<p><p>The Ultrahigh Vacuum (UHV) pumps market encompasses various types, including Ion Getter Pumps (IGPs), Titanium Sublimator Pumps (TSPs), and Non-evaporable Getters (NEG) pumps. IGPs utilize ionization to capture gases, achieving deep vacuum levels. TSPs rely on the sublimation of titanium to adsorb gas molecules effectively. NEG pumps use a chemical reaction in a getter material to reduce pressure by trapping residual gases without the need for continuous heating. These technologies are essential for applications demanding extremely low pressure environments.</p></p>

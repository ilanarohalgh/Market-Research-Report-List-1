<p><ul><li>Negative Index Metamaterial (NIM)</li><li>Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials</li><li>Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metamaterials for communication antennas encompass various types with unique properties enhancing signal functionality. Negative Index Metamaterials (NIM) enable unconventional wave propagation, improving efficiency. Single Negative (SNG) Metamaterials, characterized by either negative permittivity or permeability, enhance antenna performance by controlling electromagnetic waves. Hyperbolic Metamaterials (HMM) feature anisotropic structures, allowing for unique dispersion characteristics that optimize bandwidth and resolution. Together, these metamaterials significantly advance communication antenna design, leading to faster, more reliable wireless connectivity.</p></p>

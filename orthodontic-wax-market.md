<p><p>The orthodontic wax market includes three primary types: beeswax, paraffin wax, and carnauba wax. Beeswax is a natural wax often preferred for its softness and hypoallergenic properties, providing comfort for wearers. Paraffin wax is synthetic and known for its smooth texture, making it easy to mold and apply. Carnauba wax, derived from the leaves of the carnauba palm, offers a harder consistency and excellent sheen, enhancing durability and aesthetic appeal. Each type serves specific needs in orthodontic applications.</p></p>

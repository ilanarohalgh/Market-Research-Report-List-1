<p><strong>The Marine Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless Steel Pipe</li><li>Straight Seam Submerged Arc Welded Steel Pipe (SAW)</li><li>Straight Seam High Frequency Resistance Welded Steel Pipe (HFERW)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Pipes market encompasses various types of steel pipes critical for offshore applications. Seamless Steel Pipes are manufactured without seams, ensuring high strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for deep-water operations. Straight Seam Submerged Arc Welded Steel Pipes (SAW) offer enhanced weld integrity for large-diameter applications and are suitable for transporting oil and gas. Straight Seam High Frequency Resistance Welded Steel Pipes (HFERW) utilize high-frequency currents for welding, providing a cost-effective solution with good mechanical properties for marine environments.</p></p>

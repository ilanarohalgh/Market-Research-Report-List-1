<p><p>The RF dryers market is primarily segmented into two types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type RF dryers operate by processing specific quantities of materials in discrete batches, making them suitable for small-scale applications with varied product specifications. In contrast, continuous type RF dryers facilitate a constant flow of material, allowing for uninterrupted processing; ideal for large-scale operations that require high efficiency and consistent output. Both types leverage radio frequency energy for effective moisture removal in various industries.</p></p>

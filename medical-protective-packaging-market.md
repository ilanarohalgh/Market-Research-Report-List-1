<p><p>The medical protective packaging market encompasses three primary types: flexible packaging, rigid packaging, and foam packaging. Flexible packaging includes bags and pouches that adapt to product shapes, offering lightweight protection and barrier properties. Rigid packaging involves containers such as boxes and trays that provide sturdy protection for medical devices and pharmaceuticals. Foam packaging utilizes cushioning materials to safeguard fragile items during transit, ensuring they remain intact while preventing movement. Each type is crucial for maintaining product integrity in the healthcare sector.</p></p>

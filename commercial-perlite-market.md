<p><p>The Commercial Perlite Market comprises two primary types: Crude Form and Expanded Form. Crude Form refers to unprocessed perlite, which is mined and sold in its natural state, primarily used in construction and horticultural applications. Expanded Form, on the other hand, involves the heating of crude perlite to create lightweight, insulating particles that enhance performance in various applications like insulation, agriculture, and industrial processes. Both forms serve distinct purposes and cater to different market demands.</p></p>

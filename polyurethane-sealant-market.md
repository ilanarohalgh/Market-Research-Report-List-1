<p><strong>The Polyurethane Sealant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Multi Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane sealant market is divided into two primary types: single-component and multi-component systems. Single-component sealants are pre-packaged and ready to use, requiring no mixing, making them convenient for quick applications. They cure through moisture exposure. Multi-component sealants consist of two or more parts that must be mixed before use, allowing for greater customization in properties like curing time and adhesion. Both types are widely utilized in construction, automotive, and industrial applications for their strong bonding and flexibility.</p></p>

<p><p>The industrial fractional horsepower clutches market includes various types designed for specific applications. Overrunning clutches allow driven components to rotate freely in one direction, preventing damage during reverse rotation. Electromagnetic clutches use electrical power to engage or disengage torque transmission, offering precise control for automation. Permanent magnet clutches rely on magnetic forces to transmit power efficiently without the need for an electric current, providing smooth operation and reduced wear. Each type serves distinct functions in machinery and automotive systems.</p></p>

<p><p>The biodegradable horticultural twines market primarily features two types: PLA (Polylactic Acid) and PBAT (Polybutylene Adipate Terephthalate). PLA type twines are derived from renewable resources like cornstarch, offering compostability and a natural alternative to plastic. PBAT type twines are also biodegradable, characterized by their flexibility and strength, making them suitable for various horticultural applications. Both types are gaining popularity due to increasing environmental awareness and the demand for sustainable gardening solutions, catering to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>

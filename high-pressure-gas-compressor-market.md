<p><ul><li>Piston Compressor</li><li>Diaphragm Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-pressure gas compressor market primarily includes piston compressors and diaphragm compressors. Piston compressors utilize a reciprocating mechanism to compress gases, offering high efficiency and versatility for various applications. In contrast, diaphragm compressors use a flexible diaphragm to compress gases while providing a hermetic seal, making them suitable for handling corrosive and toxic gases. Both types cater to different industries, such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and refrigeration, driving demand for reliable and efficient gas compression solutions.</p></p>

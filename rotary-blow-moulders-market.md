<p><p>The rotary blow molders market is categorized into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer rotary blow molders produce containers from a single material layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. They are ideal for producing standard containers with consistent quality. In contrast, multi-layer rotary blow molders utilize multiple layers of different materials, enhancing functionality, barrier properties, and aesthetics. This technology is beneficial for products requiring enhanced shelf life or specific performance characteristics, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>

<p><p>The COF (Chip-on-Film) flexible package substrate market primarily consists of two types: single-layer and double-layer substrates. Single-layer substrates comprise one conductive layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, double-layer substrates feature two conductive layers, enabling more complex circuitry and enhanced performance, suitable for high-density applications like advanced electronics and displays. The choice between single and double layers depends on factors like design complexity, performance requirements, and manufacturing cost considerations.</p></p>

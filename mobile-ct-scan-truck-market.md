<p><p>The Mobile CT Scan Truck market comprises two main types: pull-type and self-propelled trucks. Pull-type mobile CT trucks are towed by another vehicle, providing flexibility and cost-effectiveness for transport. In contrast, self-propelled trucks are equipped with their own engine and drive system, allowing for autonomous operation and easier access to various locations. Both options cater to the increasing demand for portable diagnostic imaging, enhancing healthcare access in remote or underserved areas.</p></p>

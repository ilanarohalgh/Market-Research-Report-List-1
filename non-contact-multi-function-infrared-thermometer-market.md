<p><p>The non-contact multi-function infrared thermometer market includes primarily forehead type and ear type thermometers. Forehead thermometers use infrared sensors to read body temperature from the forehead without direct contact, making them ideal for quick measurements, especially in clinical settings or for children. Ear thermometers, on the other hand, measure infrared heat generated by the eardrum, providing accurate temperature readings and requiring contact with the ear. Both types offer convenience and speed, driving their popularity in healthcare and home use.</p></p>

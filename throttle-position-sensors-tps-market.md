<p><p>The Throttle Position Sensor (TPS) market is dominated by two primary types: End Switches Type and Potentiometer Type. The End Switches Type utilizes on/off switches to detect throttle position, offering simplicity and reliability in basic applications. In contrast, the Potentiometer Type employs a variable resistor to provide a continuous voltage signal, allowing for precise throttle position measurement, making it ideal for advanced engine management systems. Both types are crucial for optimizing engine performance and emissions control in automotive applications.</p></p>

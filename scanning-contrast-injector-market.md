<p><p>The scanning contrast injector market is categorized into single-chamber, two-chamber, and three-chamber types, based on the number of contrast agents they can accommodate. Single-chamber injectors are designed for administering a single contrast agent, suitable for standard imaging procedures. Two-chamber injectors allow for the simultaneous use of two different agents, enhancing imaging flexibility. Three-chamber injectors provide the capability to mix or administer three distinct agents, catering to complex diagnostic needs and improving the efficiency of imaging workflows.</p></p>

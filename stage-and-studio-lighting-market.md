<p><ul><li>Thermal Radiation Source</li><li>Discharge Light Source</li><li>Electroluminescent Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stage and studio lighting market encompasses various types of lighting technologies, including thermal radiation sources, discharge light sources, and electroluminescent lights. Thermal radiation sources, such as incandescent bulbs, emit light through heated filaments. Discharge light sources, including fluorescent and LED lights, produce illumination by passing electricity through gas or semiconductor materials. Electroluminescent lights generate light through electroluminescence, often used for flexible, thin applications. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, color rendering, and application suitability in performance environments.</p></p>

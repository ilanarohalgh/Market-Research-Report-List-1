<p><p>The Portable Thermal Scanner market encompasses three main types based on infrared wavelengths: Short-wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR is ideal for applications needing higher sensitivity and resolution in visible light and near-infrared. MWIR provides optimal performance for detecting temperature variations in a wide range of conditions, while LWIR is commonly used for thermal imaging in the industrial and security sectors, effectively sensing heat emitted from objects and ensuring accurate temperature measurements.</p></p>

<p><p>The seam tape market comprises two primary types: single-layered and multi-layered seam tapes. In contrast, multi-layered seam tapes consist of multiple layers, often enhancing durability, flexibility, and weather resistance. Multi-layered options are commonly used in high-performance industries, such as outdoor gear and construction, where superior protection against the elements is required. Each type serves distinct market needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>

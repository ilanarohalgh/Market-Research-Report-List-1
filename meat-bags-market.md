<p><p>Meat bags are essential packaging solutions designed to preserve and protect meat products. The market is divided into two primary types: plastic and paper materials. Plastic meat bags, often made from polyethylene or nylon, offer superior moisture resistance and durability, keeping meat fresh for longer. In contrast, paper meat bags are typically used for their eco-friendliness and breathability, allowing for proper airflow while showcasing the product's presentation. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
